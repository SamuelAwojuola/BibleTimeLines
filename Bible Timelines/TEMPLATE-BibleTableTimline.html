Before Creation

There was a time before creation. What we find in Genesis 1 is the beginning of this age. Quite clearly, we can see that matter already existed but was only shaped. In other words, what we have in Genesis is not the very beginning of all things, but only the beginning of this age.

No Temple

The flood destroyed Eden and its Garden which was the Tabernacle of God before it. Though God still related with men, as is evident with Melchizedek, Abraham, even all the way to Balaam, there was temple on earth until the institution of the Old Covenant in the wilderness.
Nevertheless, though there wasn't a temple until the Law, there were altars. Noah built one after the flood, and Abraham also did.
